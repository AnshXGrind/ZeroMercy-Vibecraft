Infinitus FAQ

General Information

When and where is Infinitus happening?

Infinitus 2026 will take place from **February 24th to 28th, 2026** at the **SRM University Andhra Pradesh** campus. The festival spans across multiple venues within the campus, including the main auditorium, central lawans, and various technical labs.

Who can participate in Infinitus?

Infinitus is open to all university students across India. Whether you are a tech enthusiast, a creative artist, or a budding entrepreneur, there's a spot for you. Some specific competitions may have eligibility criteria based on year of study or department.
Registrations & Teams

How do I register for events?

You can register by clicking the "SIGN IN" button to create an account. Once logged in, navigate to the **Event**, **Competition**, or **Workshop** pages and click on the specific card you're interested in to find the registration link.

Are there any registration fees?

Entry to the festival grounds is free for all SRM AP students. However, specific premium workshops and high-stakes competitions may have a nominal registration fee to cover materials and prize pools.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can register for as many events as you like! Just make sure to check the schedule on the Event pages to ensure there are no timing conflicts between your choices.
Technical & Workshops

Do I need to bring my own laptop?

For most technical workshops and hackathons, bringing your own laptop is highly recommended. Some laboratory-based sessions will provide equipment, but having your own machine allows you to keep your work and configurations.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes! All registered participants will receive e-certificates of participation. Winners of competitions will receive certificates of excellence along with their prizes.
Support

Who do I contact for more help?

If you have any further questions, you can reach out to us via the **Social Bar** on the right (X, Instagram, Discord) or visit the help desk located at the Student Technical Council office on campus.
